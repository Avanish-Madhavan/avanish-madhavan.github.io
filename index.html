A Story About Climate Change


Greetings, Earthling! My name is Ang. I have come from the Andromeda Galaxy, around 2.5 million light years away. I have travelled far in search for a planet which my kind and I can use as a new home. I must say that until now, I have found no candidate better than your Earth. Therefore, I have been tracking it for the past few centuries, in terms of your Earth years. The Earth's climate is very complicated, with many factors which influence each other. Yet there has been a steady change in the climate of the Earth, and it has many harmful effects.
I have made this website to show you my findings. You can see the climate of the Earth in different regions and how it has been steadily changing over the past few years. The temperature throughout the globe has been on a steady increase, and only Humans can fix the issue. I have found that the levels of Carbon dioxide are a major factor for this increase in temperature. This site allows you to look at the different changes and effects in different regions of the Earth. My hope is that this website will show you the urgency needed to improve the state of the Earth's climate.
